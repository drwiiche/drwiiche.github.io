# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy provides essential information regarding **iqraa** ("we," "our," or "us") in respect to user information collected through our mobile application, **NetPulse WOL: Remote PC Power**, and the use of that information.

**NetPulse WOL: Remote PC Power** is provided as a free application intended for use as is.

---

## 1. Local-First Design & Privacy
NetPulse WOL operates **100% locally on your network**.
* **No external user tracking:** We do not track user activities or behaviors.
* **No user accounts:** The application does not require you to create or log into any account.
* **No remote analytics:** We do not collect, monitor, or transmit usage statistics or analytics to external servers.
* **Private device profiles:** All host configuration files, MAC addresses, IP addresses, port lists, and settings remain stored strictly on your local device hardware.

---

## 2. Information Collection and Use
For a better experience, while using our Service, the app may require you to authorize certain device permissions. Any requested information is retained exclusively on your device and is not collected by us in any way:

*   **Location Access (Optional):** Android requires location permissions (Precise/Approximate Location and/or Background Location) to retrieve the active Wi-Fi SSID. NetPulse WOL uses this information locally to determine if you are connected to a specific Wi-Fi network before triggering Wake-on-LAN packets.
*   **Local Storage (Optional):** Used for importing or exporting host profile configurations to a JSON file.

## 4. Log Data
We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in the event of an error in the app, we may collect diagnostic data (through Google Play Services) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other crash/diagnostic statistics. This data is solely used to debug and improve application stability.
