# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy provides essential information regarding **iqraa** ("we," "our," or "us") in respect to user information collected through our game, **Solitaire Classic**, and the use of that information.

---

## 1. Personal and Non-Personal Information
We do not collect or require users to enter personal identity information (such as name, email address, or phone number) when playing **Solitaire Classic**. 

Since this game is deployed on the **Facebook Instant Games** platform, Facebook handles your core gaming credentials and user profiles. We do not store, harvest, or transmit your Facebook profile data to any external databases.

We may use third-party Advertisement SDKs or platform ads that collect device advertising IDs to show advertisements. Please refer to Facebook's core Data Policy for information regarding how they manage gameplay data.

## 2. Advertising and Platform Operations
Our game, **Solitaire Classic**, may contain ads provided through the Facebook Audience Network or Facebook Instant Games ads. These ads help us sustain and build games.

## 3. Anonymous Gameplay Data
Anonymous usage telemetry (such as game completion rates, moves count, session length, and high scores) may be logged automatically by Facebook or our analytics libraries to improve gameplay performance and game stability.

## 4. Data Security
Any correspondence you initiate with us (such as support emails) will be handled with high security and privacy. We do not sell, rent, or distribute support contacts or details to third-party marketing companies.

## 5. COPPA Compliance (Children's Privacy)
We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## 6. Amendments
We may update this policy periodically. We encourage you to review this page to keep up to date with any changes.
